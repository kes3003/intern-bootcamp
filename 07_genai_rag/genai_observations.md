## 1. Temperature Behavior
**0.2 (Low temperature)**  
- Very factual and stable  
- Minimal creativity  
- Very similar across multiple runs  
- Good for technical or scientific tasks  

**0.7 (Medium temperature)**  
- Balanced tone  
- More expressive vocabulary  
- Small variations between runs  
- Good for general writing tasks  

**1.0 (High temperature)**  
- Most creative  
- Uses metaphors, imagery, and storytelling  
- More unpredictable  
- Best for imaginative writing  

## 2. Creative vs Factual Prompts
###  Factual Prompt (Low Temp)  
- Direct, scientific explanation  
- Clear structure, accurate terminology  
- No extra decoration  
- High correctness  

###  Creative Prompt (High Temp)  
- Personification and fantasy elements  
- Rich visual descriptions  
- Emotional tone  
- Shows how temperature unlocks creativity  

## 3. Key Takeaways
- Temperature strongly affects tone and creativity.  
- Low temperature is ideal for truth-focused tasks.  
- High temperature enhances imagination and narrative quality.
