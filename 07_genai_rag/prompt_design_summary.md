# Prompt Design Summary

## Key Learnings
- Prompts act as instructions for LLMs.
- Structure, clarity, and constraints dramatically improve output quality.
- Zero-shot is fast but generic.
- Few-shot teaches the model formatting and tone.
- Chain-of-thought improves reasoning and insight depth.

## Observations
### Zero-Shot
- Summary was correct but broad.
- Output style was inconsistent.

### Few-Shot
- Produced consistent 3-line summaries.
- Matched tone and structure of examples.
- Best for customer review summarization.

### Chain-of-Thought
- Provided multi-step reasoning.
- Identified emotional tone and quality score.
- Most useful for analytics.

## Conclusion
Few-shot + clear structure gives the most reliable summaries.  
Chain-of-thought is ideal for deeper insights in customer sentiment analysis.
